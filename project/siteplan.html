Overview

Site Name

Sweet Cheonsa Art

This name represents my personal artist brand, focusing on my unique style and creative works. It's a blend of my nickname "Sweet Cheonsa"(meaning sweet "angel"(Korean for "angel")) and the word "Art" to clearly convey the artistic nature of the site.

Site Purpose

This website will serve as my personal artist portfolio, showcasing my creative projects and artwork. It aims to express my unique style, share my artistic journey, and connect with art enthusiasts.

Scenarios

1. Where can I see Sweet Cheonsa's artwork?

2. What is the inspiration behind Sweet Cheonsa's artwork?

Audience

My intended audience includes family, friends, fellow artists, and art lovers who are looking for original and expressive artwork.
I aim to connect with individuals who want to learn more about my creative process and the inspiration behind my work.
The website will be engaging for people of all ages and backgrounds who appreciate art and creativity.
